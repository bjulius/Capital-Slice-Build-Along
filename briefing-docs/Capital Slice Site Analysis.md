# Capital Slice: Site Analysis Reference

## Overview

This document provides detailed strategic analysis of the 10 permitted locations for Capital Slice food truck operations. Each site is evaluated on strengths, weaknesses, and optimal deployment conditions.

**Companion to:** `capital-slice-case-study.md`

---

## Location 1: Eastern Market

**Address:** 7th St & C St SE  
**Coordinates:** 38.8863, -76.9958  
**Category:** Market District

| Strengths | Weaknesses |
|-----------|------------|
| Weekend flea market draws 5,000+ visitors | Weather-dependent outdoor market |
| Established breakfast/brunch culture | Morning-heavy traffic (dies after 2 PM) |
| Metro accessible (Eastern Market station) | Limited afternoon event drivers |
| High-income capital Hill demographic | Competitive restaurant scene nearby |

**Optimal Conditions:** Morning positioning on fair-weather Saturdays with market operating

**Time Profile:** Strong 7 AM – 1 PM | Weak 2 PM – 8 PM

---

## Location 2: Dupont Circle

**Address:** Dupont Circle NW  
**Coordinates:** 38.9096, -77.0434  
**Category:** Metro/Urban Hub

| Strengths | Weaknesses |
|-----------|------------|
| Sunday farmers market overlap (9 AM start) | Tourist-heavy can mean slower service |
| Constant metro foot traffic | Multiple competing food options |
| Affluent neighborhood, high spending | Limited parking for restocking |
| Both breakfast and lunch viable | Evening crowds disperse |

**Optimal Conditions:** Morning through early afternoon, particularly on market days

**Time Profile:** Strong 8 AM – 3 PM | Moderate 3 PM – 6 PM | Weak after 6 PM

---

## Location 3: National Mall

**Address:** 14th St & Constitution Ave NW  
**Coordinates:** 38.8893, -77.0328  
**Category:** Tourist/Monument

| Strengths | Weaknesses |
|-----------|------------|
| Highest foot traffic in DC | Extremely weather-dependent |
| Tourist demographic willing to spend | Heavy permit competition |
| All-day viability | Tourist budget consciousness |
| Special events (rallies, festivals) | No repeat customer base |

**Optimal Conditions:** Perfect weather days, major national events, peak tourist season

**Time Profile:** Consistent 10 AM – 6 PM when weather cooperates

---

## Location 4: Georgetown Waterfront

**Address:** K St & 31st St NW  
**Coordinates:** 38.9019, -77.0637  
**Category:** Waterfront/Upscale

| Strengths | Weaknesses |
|-----------|------------|
| Scenic destination location | Highly weather-dependent |
| Weekend joggers and recreational crowds | Tourist price sensitivity |
| Upscale shopping/dining spillover | Heavy restaurant competition |
| Good weather = exceptional traffic | Parking/logistics challenging |

**Optimal Conditions:** Clear, warm Saturdays; complements rather than competes with seated dining

**Time Profile:** Strong 11 AM – 7 PM in good weather | Avoid in rain/cold

---

## Location 5: U Street Corridor

**Address:** U St & 14th St NW  
**Coordinates:** 38.9169, -77.0319  
**Category:** Arts/Music District

| Strengths | Weaknesses |
|-----------|------------|
| Vibrant nightlife spillover | Peak traffic is evening/night |
| Metro accessible | Morning traffic minimal |
| Diverse, food-adventurous demographic | Bar competition after 8 PM |
| Concert and venue proximity | Inconsistent daytime crowds |

**Optimal Conditions:** Afternoon through evening, concert nights, cultural events

**Time Profile:** Weak morning | Moderate 2 PM – 5 PM | Strong 5 PM – 9 PM

---

## Location 6: Convention Center

**Address:** 9th St & Mt Vernon Pl NW  
**Coordinates:** 38.9048, -77.0229  
**Category:** Convention/Business

| Strengths | Weaknesses |
|-----------|------------|
| Major conventions = 5,000–15,000 captive attendees | Dead when no events scheduled |
| Weather-independent (indoor venue crowds) | Weekend conventions less common |
| Business crowd = higher per-transaction spending | Convention food vendors compete |
| Lunch rush extremely concentrated | Limited street-level access |

**Optimal Conditions:** Exclusively on major convention days—requires calendar monitoring

**Time Profile:** Binary—either excellent (event day) or zero (no event)

**Critical Note:** Must verify convention schedule weekly. Do not deploy without confirmed event.

---

## Location 7: Capital One Arena

**Address:** 7th St & F St NW  
**Coordinates:** 38.8981, -77.0209  
**Category:** Sports/Entertainment Venue

| Strengths | Weaknesses |
|-----------|------------|
| Wizards, Mystics, Capitals games = 18,000 capacity | Schedule-dependent; dead on non-game days |
| Weather-independent (indoor event crowds) | Arena vendors compete for pre/post game |
| Pre-game hunger window (2–3 hours before tip/puck drop) | Some games have low attendance |
| Pizza + sports = natural pairing | Evening games limit dinner window |

**Optimal Conditions:** Game days, especially afternoon starts; bad weather makes indoor events more attractive

**Time Profile:** Peaks 2–3 hours before game time through 1 hour after

**Weather Interaction:** Bad weather INCREASES value (indoor event becomes more appealing)

---

## Location 8: Columbia Heights

**Address:** 14th St & Irving St NW  
**Coordinates:** 38.9283, -77.0326  
**Category:** Residential/Neighborhood

| Strengths | Weaknesses |
|-----------|------------|
| Metro plaza creates gathering point | Lower foot traffic than commercial districts |
| Residential weekend brunch culture | Residential = more price-conscious |
| Less direct food truck competition | No major event drivers |
| Good for testing new menu items | Better backup than primary |

**Optimal Conditions:** Low-risk positioning when primary locations are compromised; neighborhood events

**Time Profile:** Moderate 10 AM – 4 PM | Weak outside that window

**Strategic Use:** Backup location. Deploy when weather or events make premium locations risky.

---

## Location 9: H Street Corridor

**Address:** H St & 12th St NE  
**Coordinates:** 38.9001, -76.9931  
**Category:** Arts/Festival District

| Strengths | Weaknesses |
|-----------|------------|
| Major street festivals draw 4,000+ | Inconsistent non-festival traffic |
| Streetcar line delivers foot traffic | Better evening than daytime |
| Growing food/arts destination | Strong restaurant competition |
| Revitalized area with spending power | Event-dependent performance |

**Optimal Conditions:** H Street Festival days; cultural events; evening positioning

**Time Profile:** Weak morning | Moderate afternoon | Strong evening on event days

**Critical Note:** H Street Festival is a major annual event—prioritize this location when scheduled.

---

## Location 10: Navy Yard / The Wharf

**Address:** Half St SE (Navy Yard) / Maine Ave SW (Wharf)  
**Coordinates:** 38.8769, -77.0074 (Navy Yard) / 38.8783, -77.0253 (Wharf)  
**Category:** Waterfront/Sports

| Strengths | Weaknesses |
|-----------|------------|
| Nationals Park proximity (41,000 capacity) | Dead when no games/events |
| Wharf development = dining destination | Game schedule dependency |
| Waterfront atmosphere | Weather impacts outdoor seating crowds |
| Pre-game crowds hungry and willing to spend | Heavy competition from Wharf restaurants |

**Optimal Conditions:** Nationals game days, afternoon starts, waterfront events

**Time Profile:** Peaks 2–3 hours before first pitch through 7th inning

**Seasonal Note:** MLB season (April–October) only for Nationals-driven traffic. Wharf provides year-round baseline but with higher competition.

## Quick Reference: Optimal Pairings

**Fair Weather, No Major Events:**
- Morning: Eastern Market + Dupont Circle
- Afternoon: National Mall + Georgetown Waterfront

**Rainy Day, Indoor Events Available:**
- All Day: Capital One Arena + Convention Center

**Major Sports Game (Afternoon Start):**
- Morning: Eastern Market or Dupont Circle
- Afternoon: Navy Yard (relocate) + Capital One Arena

**Festival Day (H Street):**
- Morning: Eastern Market
- Afternoon: H Street Corridor (relocate) + stay at morning winner
